About Us

The USDU is aimed to facilitating healthy discourse and discussions on campus. Our mission is to create events and spaces where people can intellectually spar with one another and test their whit whilst building their rhetoric skills.

Our vision is to break away as many echo-chambers as possible by providing spaces of contact and critical engagement. We hope you will join and engage with us in that vision.

Debating in Action

Why Join Us?

Critical Thinking

Develop Critical Thinking

Enhance your ability to analyze complex issues and form well-reasoned arguments. Our debates cover a wide range of topics that will challenge your thinking and expand your knowledge.

Public Speaking

Improve Public Speaking

Gain confidence in expressing your ideas clearly and persuasively in front of an audience. Regular practice in a supportive environment will help you overcome nervousness and develop your own style.

Diverse Perspectives

Expand Your Perspectives

Engage with diverse viewpoints and develop a deeper understanding of social, political, and philosophical topics. Our members come from various backgrounds and disciplines, creating a rich environment for intellectual growth.
